Fresh Garden Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed greens (lettuce, spinach, arugula)
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup sliced cucumbers
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/4 cup sliced red onion
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Wash and dry all vegetables.
  2. Chop the lettuce, slice the cucumbers, tomatoes, and onions.
  3. In a large bowl, combine all the vegetables.
  4. Drizzle olive oil and balsamic vinegar over the salad.
  5. Toss gently to mix everything together.
  6. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Top with feta cheese if desired and serve fresh!
