Delicious Homemade Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (24 oz) jar of marinara sauce
  • 1 (15 oz) container ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions, then drain.
  3. In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté onions and garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add ground beef, cook until browned, then drain excess fat.
  5. Stir in marinara sauce and simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, salt, and pepper.
  7. In a baking dish, layer sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella, and repeat.
  8. Top with Parmesan cheese and bake for 35-40 minutes until golden and bubbly.
  9. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
